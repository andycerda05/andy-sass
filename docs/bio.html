Band Member Bios

Harvey Dale

Harvey Dale, 43, is the lead singer of the band. He met the other members of the band at a bar, where he impressed them with his vocals and was recruited to join. They've made several hit songs since then, most notably Hot Bottom Gals and Radio Madonna.

Donald Trunk

Donald Trunk, 74, is the main drummer for the band. Trunk joined the band after losing his job in January, and unfortunately has been banned from Twitter after sparking a riot at the Texas Capitol.

Moneybag Joe

Joe Byden (or Moneybag Joe), 78, is the main guitarist for the band. Fun fact: Byden currently owes everyone in America $1400 (well, mostly everyone).
